Our Story

A Living Sanctuary
of the Earth

Our Sustainable Living Farm is a nature-based experiential space designed to help families, professionals, and organisations reconnect with soil, food, traditional wisdom, and mindful living.

Located in the heart of agricultural Karnataka, the farm features a traditional mud house, natural farming areas, forest-inspired green spaces, and community learning zones — all carefully curated for transformative experiences.

NGO Initiatives

Ammana Prakruti Madilu – which means “Mother Nature’s Lap” – is a peaceful natural retreat created for people to reconnect with nature, simplicity, and traditional rural life. Surrounded by greenery, fresh air, and the calm sounds of birds and wind, this place offers a refreshing escape from the busy city lifestyle. At Ammana Prakruti Madilu, visitors can experience the beauty of nature through farm walks, outdoor games, traditional village activities, and relaxing moments under open skies. The farm promotes sustainable living, respect for nature, and a healthy lifestyle close to the earth. Families, children, and nature lovers can enjoy a variety of experiences such as exploring the farm, learning about plants and farming, playing in open spaces, and participating in fun weekend activities. The environment is designed to be safe, natural, and joyful, making it an ideal place for relaxation, learning, and memorable gatherings. Ammana Prakruti Madilu is not just a farm — it is a space where nature nurtures the mind, body, and soul.
